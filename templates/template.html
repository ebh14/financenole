The very first thing you do after you've been offered a job or are beginning to manage your finances more is budget. Budgeting allows you to see what and where exactly your money is going. You'll never realize you spent $250 on fast-food last month until you budget or your card gets declined and $50 of overdraft fees are applied to your account. While budgeting helps with reducing excess it also allows you to set reasonable goals. It makes clear to you that you can't afford that new computer/car/russian bride.
