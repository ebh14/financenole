Saving Tips

Save at home:

One of the best ways to help your financial situation is to look at your home lifestyle.Certain activities impact

your energy bill. A little known fact about energy cost is the existence of on and off peak hours.

In parts of Tallahassee, off peak hours are from 7p.m - 7a.m and weekends. During this time energy costs less,

so wait until 7p.m to fire up the bubble bath. Another power hog is incandescent bulbs. Even though they are outlawed,

they still seem to show up in rental houses. Consider switching to LED or fluorescent bulbs for overtime savings.


Save day to day:

When it comes time to fork over some cash, how you have your money stored can make a difference.

Multiple accounts can discourage splurges and overspending. Instead of having one account stock piled

with all your funds, having some funds in savings and multiple checking accounts as well as cash can

help divvy up money for bills, leisure, emergency, etc. Just ensure you have enough in your bill fund

because the 1st seems to roll around all to sudden. By using this technique, it gets rid of the over-

indulging mentality. For instance, when you have a whole bag of cookies in your lap on the couch, you

will likely consume the whole thing, but if you take a few cookies from the kitchen and sit down, the

inconvenience of a second trip will likely discourage over-indulging.


Save on purchases:

Ecommerce is flourishing more than ever which means you save more. The web is a great tool in helping

your budget because it allows you to compare prices; something stores do not want you to do. Ordering

online, although makes you anxious, can save you big for several reasons. If the seller is based in a

different state, you do not have to pay taxes on the purchase. Some websites, like eBay or Sierra Trading

Post, allow you to purchased used which is not the case in most stores saving you a little bit more plus

you do not have to deal with craigslist creepers. One perk not usually not available purchasing in store is

coupons. Just subscribe to the newsletter and usually they send coupon codes right to your e-mail. Even

groceries are being sold online now on websites like Jet. Shopping online is an easy way to keep your

savings stacked.
